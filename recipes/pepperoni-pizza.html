Pepperoni Pizza

Cheesy, buttery, meaty with aromatic sauce!

If this is your first time ever making your own pizza, no worries!
With only a few simple steps - you'll end up with a restaurant-style
pizza within the comfort of your own home. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Active yeast
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Peeled, canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pepperonis
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix 1 tbsp of active yeast to 1 cup of water and allow to foam.
  2. Once foamy, add 2 cups of flour and mix. Slowly pour in 2 tbsp each of olive oil
    and melted butter as you mix. Set aside and allow dough to rise.
  3. Chop and slightly brown onions in pan. Added chopped garlic - salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Remove about 5-6 peeled tomatoes from can and put into blender. Add in onion mixture,
    1 tbsp of basil, 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning, and a spoonful of tomato paste to thicken sauce.
  5. Carefully pulse blender. Not too much as it will make the sauce to thin.
  6. By this time, your dough should be risen. Go ahead and preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
  7. Press out dough to preference. I like to leave a thick crust.
  8. Once the dough has been pressed to your liking, spread your freshly made pizza sauce evenly onto dough.
  9. Spread mozzarella evenly on pizza.
  10. Add pepperonis on top of cheese. Once you've added your pepperonis, you're ready to stick it into the oven!
  11. Put pizza into oven at 500 degrees and allow to bake for 12 minuntes.
  12. Allow pizza to cool, cut into even slices, and enjoy!
